MY VISITS

Hawassa

Hawassa is one of the most beautiful cities in Ethiopia, located in the Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region (SNNPR). It is known for its stunning lakes and mountains.

The city is also home to historical and cultural attractions. I had the opportunity to visit Hawassa to attend my cousin's graduation from Hawassa University.

Sodere Resort

In my childhood, I eagerly waited for school breaks just to go to this place. Sodere Resort offers a mix of exhilarating water activities and tranquil experiences, surrounded by lush greenery.

Whether you're seeking a relaxing getaway or an action-packed adventure, Sodere Resort has something for everyone. It's the perfect place to create lasting memories.
